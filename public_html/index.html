Welcome to Habitat for Humanity in London, Ontario, Canada.
With Habitat, 100% of your donation goes towards building homes. Please give generously to help us build a better community.

Build 2009 is coming soon!

Volunteers should download the volunteer application, complete it in full, and fax or mail it to us. Once we have more information on this year's build (times, locations), we will post it on the website. At the same time we will start to mail out registration packages to anyone that has already submitted an application.

Points to remember:
  • Applicants are accepted on a first-come first-serve basis.
  • You must be 18 years of age to volunteer.
  • Applicants 16-17 years of age may volunteer if accompanied by an adult.
