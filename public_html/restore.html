The ReStore sells new and slightly used renovation materials and supplies for at least 50% off retail.

You'll find lots of materials in our showroom, including
  • Furniture
  • Appliances
  • Cabinetry
  • Bathroom & Kitchen Fixtures
  • Doors & Windows
  • Flooring
  • Heating Fixtures
  • Lumber & Trim
  • Hardware
Everything sold in the ReStore has been donated by companies and individuals who want to help Habitat for Humanity London. Thanks to donors, volunteers and recycling, we are able to sell everything for a low price.

Hours, Directions

The ReStore is open for business Mon-Fri 9-5:30 and Sat 9-4 except on holidays. Reach us by phone at 519-659-1949, by email, or visit our London ReStore showroom located at:
Unit 6, 45 Pacific Ct,
London, ON, N5V 3N4.

Donate

To donate materials, simply give us a call at 519-659-1949. The ReStore needs new and used building materials that are resalable, repairable and/or reusable.
